THE COOK MOB

The winners will be selected based on 70% juries decision and 30% likes





Make sure to deliver creative healthy and tasty dishes
Can use non-electric equipments like mixers, chopper, juicer, and graters. Ready-made décor items are not allowed.
Sculptures must be entirely made from fruits and/or vegetables and may not contain flowers. Pieces are to be made entirely of edible food without plastic supports in frames.
Using fresh flowers or lights are not allowed on the dish. Everything must be made and baked by the person. All decorations should be edible.


RULES

  • 1. Individual participation
  • 2. Participants may use either photos of their dish or videos of their preparation
  • 3. Photography should contain 25% of initial stage of cooking. 50% of partially cooked food and a completely finished food
  • 4. Photos should be smaller than 700 MB
  • 5. Video length shouldn’t exceed 3 mins, and size shouldn’t exceed 700 MB.
  • 6. Prepare your dish with creative ideas to earn marks for creativity in the dish.

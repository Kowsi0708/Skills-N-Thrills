CRAFT N SKILLS

The winners will be selected based on 70% juries decision and 30% likes





Participants will show their creativity and take a two-minute video. Waste material could be anything like tetra packs, bottles, newspapers, old utensils, jute material or any second-hand items that otherwise would be thrown away.
Models must be styled with hair and costume. You can transform people's appearance via make-up, paint, wigs, and other accessories.
You can use gemstone, crystal, seed beads, pearls Or any other items to finish the product
You can carve using soap, pen, pencil and vegetables
Only mehandi is allowed. No other materials should be used. Participants have to apply mehandi on the palm as well as dorsal side of the hand. Participants can apply mehandi on their own hand or any other person’s hand.
You can use bucket, Paper towels, Ribbon, cardstock, scrapbook paper, tissue paper, metallic text-weight paper, and crepe paper. You'll be using these materials to make DIY paper flowers like roses, peonies, dahlias, anemones, cherry blossoms, and other pretty flowers that you'll love creating.
You can use clay to make different sizes of clay pots.
You can use papers to make art of folding papers into attractive shapes to make decorative objects.


RULES

  • 1. Convert your idea into a product (may be art/craft/useful things)
  • 2. A video can also be considered as an entry provided it is very clear and formal
  • 3. Participants are allowed to submit maximum 3 crafts
  • 4. Craft must be original and created entirely by student

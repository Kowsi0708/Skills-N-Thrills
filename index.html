Why Skills 'N Thrills What is Skills 'N Thrills about ?

Skills N thrills is a virtual event conducted by the Self Development Club of Kongu Engineering College (KEC) for both students and faculty (teaching and non-teaching staffs). With a grand success of Skills N thrills season 1, 2 and 3, season 4 is aiming to be much bigger than ever with lots of new events. It acknowledges and celebrates the talents of every individual in KEC.

EXPLORE YOUR POTENTIALS

The History

Skills 'N thrills season 3 witnessed the great achievements of our staff -Mrs.Indhumathi (MBA),won caption writing, Mr.Thangavel (CHEM),won Poet’s café, Shanmugapriya won Mehandi. Sandhiya(CHEM) and Srinivasan(MECH) were honoured with the “Find of the Year”.

01

About the last season

Season 3 was a great success and it also received positive feedbacks with a wide reach in KEC, had 450+ participants with extraordinary talents exhibited and were recognised by the fellow mates. Participants showcased their talents in various events and entertained the viewers.

02

Special in Season-4

Season 4 aims at giving surplus of opportunities to the participants, hence becoming better and better. Various creative events are added to unleash and appreciate the talents. Cultural event is added exclusively for the those who are interested in singing, dance and music.

03

Have Fun

Opportunities are keys to exhibit talent. Let’s not worry about what others might think and do what we love to do through SDC's grand event Skills 'N thrills season 4. Unleash your talents and get acknowledged by your peers, this grand virtual intra college event is for you.

Go Green

Go Green
"GO GREEN BEFORE THE GREEN GOES"Go Green event of SDC aims at raising public awareness of environmental degradation and to encourage students about the green behaviour. Showcasing the best ideas for replacement of plastics through art/craft. eg: decorative items, paper straws, cloth bags etc. Products must be made by own (not purchased items/ photo from internet). This opportunity acts as bringing out the creative crafting skills of the students. Every participant's responsible act of going towards greenery is provided with an e-certificate and best ideas has been featured in official Instagram page of SDC.
